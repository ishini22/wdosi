Beaches




UUnawatuna(Jungle Beach)


Unawatuna Beach is certain to figure on any list of Sri Lanka's top beaches. This beach, located about an hour from Colombo, Sri Lanka's capital city, is a beach lover's paradise. Unawatuna beach is a terrific place to unwind and get away from the usual, with elegant restaurants, pubs, shacks, beach houses, retail lanes, and exciting activities to partake in in the surrounding regions.
The beach features golden sand and lovely water and is shaped like a banana. The thick palm palms here not only add to the beauty of the beach, but also help to keep the environment comfortable on most days.


Marble Beach


>Another beautiful beach in Sri Lanka is Marble Beach, commonly known as Marble Bay Beach. The beach is located in Marble Bay, which is part of Trincomalee's natural harbor. When the sun shines brightly and the water is calm, the surface gleams like marble, which is how the name came to be.


Bentota Beach


>Bentota is around 64 kilometers (40 miles) south of Colombo and 101 kilometers (63 miles) south of Bandaranaike International Airport. It is mostly a rural region with big coconut palm palms dominating the landscape. The Colombo-Galle major road (A2) and the Colombo-Galle-Matara Southern Railway line both lead to it.
