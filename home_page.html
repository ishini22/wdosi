Welcome to Sri Lanaka


Sri Lanka, originally Ceylon, is an Indian Ocean island republic separated from peninsular India by the Palk Strait.
Due to its compactness, Sri Lanka is also a nation of astounding contrast and magnificent beauty.
Visitors may enjoy spectacular beaches, safari trips, guided walks through historic cities, and a taste of contemporary Sri Lanka along the coast.

Places to Visit


Kandy


Kandy is a big city in Sri Lanka's central region. It's located on a plateau surrounded by mountains with tea plantations and a rich rainforest. The gorgeous Kandy Lake (Bogambara Lake), which is famous for strolling, is the city's center. The Temple of the Tooth is one of Kandy's most revered Buddhist sites.



Trincomalee


Trincomalee is a port city on Sri Lanka's northeast coast. Fort Frederick was erected by the Portuguese in the 17th century on a peninsula. The majestic Koneswaram Temple rests on Swami Rock rock, a favorite vantage point for blue-whale spotting, within its grounds.


Nuwara Eliya


Nuwara Eliya is a city in central Sri Lanka's tea country hills. Roses and tree ferns abound in the organically planted Hakgala Botanical Gardens, which also serve as a haven for monkeys and blue magpies. A colorful Hindu shrine nearby, Seetha Amman Temple, is ornamented with holy figures.
