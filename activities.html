Activities to do in Sri Lanka

Things to do


Hiking

Sri Lanka is a unique hiking location. There are trails for all levels of skill, from gentle strolls to strenuous hikes. There are around 400 waterfalls in the nation, and many hikes involve a visit to one of them.

Climb Sigiriya


A visit to this location will let you to see King Kasyapa's 1600-year-old Sigiriya Fortress. Two moats and three ramparts surround this huge property. It was previously King Kasyapa's private house and is now one of Sri Lanka's most popular tourist destinations. This is one of the greatest tourist destinations in Sri Lanka if you are interested in ancient sites.

Ella


The town of Ella has to be one of Sri Lanka's most stunning surroundings. Apart from the wonderful natural beauty that attracts visitors from all over the world, the town boasts a lively atmosphere with excellent cafes and restaurants. Head to one of the bars on the main street if you're searching for a vibrant evening.


Safari

Due to its wealth of national parks, rich wildlife – including elephants and leopards – and sustainable conservation approach, Sri Lanka has become one of the most sought-after safari destinations in the world.

Udawalawe


Udawalawe National Park is a national park in Sri Lanka that is on the border of the provinces of Sabaragamuwa and Uva. The park was established to provide a safe haven for wild creatures displaced by the Udawalawe Reservoir's construction on the Walawe River, as well as to safeguard the reservoir's catchment area.

Yala


Yala National Park, which borders the Indian Ocean, is Sri Lanka's most visited and second largest national park. There are five blocks in the park, two of which are now available to the public, as well as bordering parks.


Whale Watching

Sri Lanka is quickly establishing itself as one of the best spots in the world to watch both blue and sperm whales, which can be seen off the island's southern coast. Between December and April, when the sea is calm and these magnificent beasts pass through Sri Lankan waters, is the perfect time to visit.

Mirissa


One of the most thrilling water activities you can enjoy while on vacation in Sri Lanka is whale and dolphin watching in Mirissa. Blue whales are the world's largest creatures, and Mirissa is the ideal destination in Sri Lanka for whale and dolphin watching cruises.
